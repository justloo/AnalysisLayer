## Precondition A. The scoreboard's "expected forecast" field cannot currently be trusted.

This is the most urgent finding in the whole exercise, because it is not a reasoning defect, it is a defect in how success is measured, and it has now hidden two real failures behind a manufactured pass.

**First occurrence.** In the mechanism battery, the report's `weak_signal_early_cut` card displayed Expected Forecast as `no_change` and Actual Forecast as `no_change`, marked PASS. The scenario file's `expectations.leading_hypothesis` is `price_cut`. The same report's own embedded ground-truth narrative for that scenario ends with "the material action in the window is a price cut on the Starter line," directly contradicting the Expected Forecast field displayed two paragraphs above it.
